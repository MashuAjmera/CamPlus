About


Mashu Ajmera

Front End Designer

Mashu Ajmera is the front-end developer and co-founder of CamPlus, a web app that keeps track of all your upcoming events, gets lecture details, plan your trips according to the best bus that fits you, reminds for mess meals. He is responsible for the company's overall vision and strategy as well as day-to-day operations.

Since the beginning, Mashu has focused on simplicity and inspiring creativity through solving problems with thoughtful product design. As a result, CamPlus has become the home for every college students' needs.

A native of Guwahati, India, Mashu is pursuing B. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from LNMIIT, Jaipur. When he is not writing scripts, he is writing a blog or spending time with his family and friends.
